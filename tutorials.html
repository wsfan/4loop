1. Google Chrome development toolsOriginally presented by Piri on Friday, 20th June.

Google Chrome has in-built development tools that aid the creation and editing of webpages.
Access the tools by opening Google Chrome and following through to: View > Tools > Dev Tools (or on a Mac, View > Developer > Development Tools.
